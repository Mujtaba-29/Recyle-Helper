About Recycle Helper

Recycle Helper leverages advanced AI models such as YOLOv8 and SegFormer to identify waste items from images and provide actionable recycling suggestions. Empower your sustainability efforts with intelligent recommendations.

Key Features

  • Upload images to detect waste items with advanced AI models
  • Receive eco-friendly and practical recycling suggestions
  • Calculate carbon footprint reduction for each recycling activity
  • User-friendly and interactive design
